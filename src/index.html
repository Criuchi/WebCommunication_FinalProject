Knobbyâ€™s Kids Hockey was founded in 2006 by friends, Knobby Knudsen, Jerry Slavik and Frank Spry. Our founders recognized the need for sports for children who may not otherwise be able to join a league. The initial season saw 50 kids hit the ice over two hours on Saturday mornings.
